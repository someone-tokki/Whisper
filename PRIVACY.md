# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Whisper is a local media player for iOS. It is designed to play and organize audio, video, and subtitle files stored on your device.

## Data Collection

Whisper does not collect personal data.

The app does not require an account, does not include analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not track users across apps or websites.

## Local Files

Whisper reads media and subtitle files that you place in the app's local document folder through the iOS Files app or other local file workflows.

Your audio, video, subtitle, playlist, and playback progress data stay on your device. Whisper does not upload your media files to a server.

## On-Device Settings

Whisper may store local settings and playback progress on your device so the app can remember preferences such as playback position, subtitle settings, appearance, and library state.

These settings are stored locally and are not sent to the developer.

## Third-Party Services

Whisper does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Whisper does not knowingly collect data from children or any other users.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated when app behavior changes. The latest version will be published in this repository.

## Contact

For privacy questions, please use the support channel linked from the App Store product page or open an issue in this repository.

# 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 16 日

Whisper 是一款 iOS 本地媒体播放器，用于播放和整理存储在你设备上的音频、视频和字幕文件。

## 数据收集

Whisper 不收集个人数据。

本 App 不需要账号，不包含分析或广告 SDK，也不会跨 App 或网站追踪用户。

## 本地文件

Whisper 会读取你通过 iOS “文件”App 或其他本地文件流程放入 App 本地文档目录中的媒体和字幕文件。

你的音频、视频、字幕、播放列表和播放进度数据会保留在设备本地。Whisper 不会将你的媒体文件上传到服务器。

## 设备本地设置

Whisper 可能会在设备本地保存设置和播放进度，以便记住播放位置、字幕设置、外观偏好和资料库状态等信息。

这些设置仅保存在设备本地，不会发送给开发者。

## 第三方服务

Whisper 不使用第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

## 儿童隐私

Whisper 不会有意收集儿童或其他用户的数据。

## 政策变更

当 App 行为发生变化时，本隐私政策可能会更新。最新版本会发布在本仓库中。

## 联系方式

如有隐私相关问题，请使用 App Store 产品页提供的技术支持渠道，或在本仓库中提交 issue。
